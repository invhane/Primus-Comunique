Mission

We, the community of Merryland Montessori and High School Inc., commit ourselves to the formation of value-laden Christian Filipino graduates sensitive & responsive to the needs of others.

Vision

We envision ourselves to be an institution where foundation begins to produce graduates imbued with Christian Filipino values committed for human service.

Philosophy

Merryland Montessori and High School believes that education is a unique learning experience for each child facilitated by the community & learning institution to grow and mature emotionally, intellectually, physically, socially, and spiritually.
